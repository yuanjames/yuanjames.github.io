CMA-ES and TPE are two advanced optimisation methods currently which have been used to solve HPO problems.

The main idea of CMA-ES is multivariate distribution, with evolution strategy. After each generation evaluated, the distribution will be updated by their novel ideas.

In contrast, TPE makes use of surrogate model to approximate the real situation, meanwhile, draws candidates and select the most promosing one to be evaluated.
