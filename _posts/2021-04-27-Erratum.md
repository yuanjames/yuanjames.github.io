Erratum for A Systematic Comparison Study on Hyperparameter Optimisation of Graph Neural Networks for Molecular Property Prediction


There are two typos in the paper: A Systematic Comparison Study on Hyperparameter Optimisation of Graph Neural Networks for Molecular Property Prediction. The paper is published in GECCO 2021.

In Algorithm 1, U means uniform distribution rather than normal distribution. It is a typo error; in the paper, Section 2.1 gives the explanation for uniform distribution.

In the Section 4.1 Experimental Settings, the significance level should be 10% rather than 5% (i.e, 5% is for a side, so the significance level should 10% when two-tailed test is applied). However, this error does not affect experimental results.
